Original Iced Coffee

A refreshing cold drink made with freshly brewed coffee, blended with sweet cane syrup and creamy milk. Served over ice in a clear cup, this drink is smooth, rich, and perfectly balanced between bold coffee flavor and sweet creaminess. Ideal for hot days and coffee lovers who enjoy their caffeine chilled and delicious!

Tim Hortons Original Blend Hot Coffee

A warm, aromatic cup of Tim Hortons’ signature Original Blend coffee. This medium roasted brew is crafted from 100% premium Arabica beans, delivering a balanced body with a smooth finish and rich flavor in every sip.

Tim Hortons Iced Cappuccino

A signature creamy, blended frozen coffee beverage topped with whipped cream. Made by combining Tim Hortons’ rich espresso base with milk, ice, and sweet syrup, then blending to a smooth, frothy consistency. Served chilled in a clear cup, this drink delivers a refreshing, indulgent flavor that’s both bold and sweet. Perfect for those looking for a coffee-slushie hybrid with a velvety finish.

Tim Hortons Caramel Iced Capp

Indulge in a creamy, caramel-flavored twist on the iconic Tim Hortons Iced Capp. This blended frozen coffee, enriched with caramel syrup and topped with whipped cream and a luscious caramel drizzle, offers a sweet, refreshing treat served chilled in a clear cup. Perfect for fans of dessert-style coffee drinks!

Tim Hortons Black Iced Coffee

A crisp, bold iced coffee brewed from Tim Horton’s Original Blend—100% premium Arabica beans—served over ice in a clear cup. No milk or sweeteners added, letting the pure coffee flavor shine through with a refreshing, slightly nutty finish.
